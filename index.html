About Me

I am a staff research engineer with Chunghwa Telecom Laboratories in Taiwan. I received the M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (Signal Processing) from National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Chun (Jeremy) Lee. I am interesting in machine learning, big data analysis, and computer vision.

My recent efforts focus on Resident Evil 7.
